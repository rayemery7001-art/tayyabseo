📌 Understanding SEO

What Is Local SEO and Why Does It Matter?

Local SEO is the process of making your business visible to people searching for your services in a specific location. When someone types "plumber near me" or "AC repair London," Google shows a map with three business listings — that is the Google Map Pack, and appearing there is the primary goal.

3+ Years Local SEO Experience

Local intent drives purchase behaviour

Research shows that over 76% of people who search for something nearby visit a related business within 24 hours. If your business is not appearing in local results, you are handing those customers directly to competitors.

❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Local SEO and how is it different from regular SEO? +
Local SEO focuses specifically on improving your business's visibility for location-based searches — "near me" queries and city-specific searches. Regular SEO targets broader national or global keywords. Local SEO optimises your Google Business Profile, local citations, and location pages, while regular SEO focuses more on domain authority and content marketing.
How long does Local SEO take to show results? +
Most clients start seeing improvements in 2–4 months, with significant results appearing by month 4–6. Local SEO is an ongoing process — the longer you invest, the stronger your position becomes. Google Business Profile optimisations can show results faster, sometimes within weeks.
